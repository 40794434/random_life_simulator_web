How to Survive Life (May Be)

Welcome to Random Life Simulator. Your life will start at age 18 with absolute no control over your starting stats. Just like the real life. Good Luck!

Step 1: Accept Your Fate

Your character begins with randomly generated stats: Happiness, Wealth, Health, and Intelligence. They might be amazing. They might be tragic. There are no refunds.

Step 2: Click "New Year"

Each click advances one year of your character’s life. A random event will occur. Some events will boost your stats. Some will ruin everything. That’s the fun part.

Step 3: Make Questionable Life Decisions

Sometimes, you’ll be given choices. Choose wisely… or don’t. Your decisions will affect your stats — and possibly your lifespan.

How do You Die?

  • Health drops below 20
  • You reach age 85+
  • A truly unlucky random event

When you die, you'll receive a summary of your life. Then you join the Graveyard. Forever.

There is No Winning.

This game has no victory condition. Life is unpredictable. Your goal is simple: See what kind of story unfolds.
